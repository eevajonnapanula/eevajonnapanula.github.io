About

Hey! My name is Eevis, and I'm a software developer from Finland. My favourite technologies right now are React, TypeScript, GraphQL and Clojure.

I love sharing what I have learned, and one of the best moments of my career have been those, when I can help someone to get excited about programming. I've been part of teams organizing beginner level coding workshops, and also have done some talks in local meetups.

When not coding, I play ultimate frisbee in national level, and hopefully next summer in World Ultimate And Guts Championships representing Finland.
